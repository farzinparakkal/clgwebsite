IPT & GPTC SHORANUR


IPT & GPTC (Instituion of Printing Technology and Government Polytechnic College) is an Instituion in Palakkad district. It was established in the year 1986 under the Directorate of Technical Education, Govt. of Kerala, with noble vision of empowering the people by offering good quality of technical education. The polytechnic college is approved by AICTE, New Delhi and offer three diploma programs in engineering and technology.

The campus is located at Red gate in Shoranur Taluk, Palakkad district, spread 38 acres of land of panoramic greenery. The river Nila is around 10 km away from the college. Thirty seven years of exemplary performance has established our college as a centre of excellence and is one of the most reputed institutions of Kerala. Apart from the regular courses, evening diploma course, vocational courses are also offered here.
